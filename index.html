Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yum Goong is a classic thai soup renown for its bold flavors and aromatic spices. this hot and tower soap features a tantallzing blend of lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves, combined with succulent shrimp.

"The key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors sweet, sour, salty, and spicy." chef somchai

ingredients

  1. Lemongrass: 2 stalks
  2. Thai basil: 1 cup
  3. Kaffir lime leaves: 3 leaves
  4. shrimp: 500kg

prepearation:

  • Simmer the broth for 10 minutes.
  • Add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves.
  • Stir in shrim and cook until pink
  • Season with fish sauce, lime juice, and chill paste.
  • Garnish with Thai basil and serve hot.
    •

    Cooking Tips

    • 1/4 cup of fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavors.
    • 1 tablespoon of chill paste gives the soup it's signature heat.
